What to do next

  1. Message the other neighbor in the app and share what you're hoping for. Most issues end here.
  2. Keep your evidence together — photos, receipts, and your in-app messages.
  3. If it isn't resolved, submit this record to NSA at [Insert Email] or tap “Report a concern” in the app. We review it against our policies.

Neighbor Service App is a neutral marketplace. We provide tools to document and report issues, but we don't mediate disputes or guarantee outcomes — see Terms, Section 12. Nothing is sent to NSA until you submit your record.

Knowing the boundaries

What NSA Can & Can't Do

What we can do

  • Give you tools to document and report an issue
  • Review reports against our platform policies
  • Remove reviews or content that break the rules
  • Suspend or terminate accounts for violations, fraud, or safety concerns
  • Point you to the right next step

What we can't do

  • Mediate or decide disputes between users
  • Guarantee a refund, redo, or any specific outcome
  • Control or recover payments made off-platform
  • Take responsibility for services, property access, or interactions between users
  • Act as your legal representative
