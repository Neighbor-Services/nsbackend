Trust & safety

We check, so you
don't have to wonder.

Every provider completes a third-party background screening — before activation, no exceptions. On most platforms, background checks are optional, inconsistent, or reserved for providers who pay for a badge. We built Neighbor Service the other way around. Screening isn't a premium feature here — it's the front door.

Pending registration

A provider signs up and builds a profile. It isn't visible to anyone yet.

Check submitted

The provider completes their screening through Checkr, an independent third-party screening company.

Check in review

Results are reviewed. Approval is subject to passing.

Approved provider

Only now does the profile go live — carrying the badge you'll see on every provider, every time.

What screening covers — and what it can't.

Background screening is a serious, independent check of a provider's history — and it's required of everyone here. It's also not a crystal ball, and we won't pretend otherwise. That's why screening is only the first layer: every job is also verified with a Security Code from arrival to completion, every review comes from a real completed job, and our team reviews every report. Trust isn't one checkbox. It's a system — and you're protected by all of it.
